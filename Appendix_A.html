Appendix A - Dataset

Appendix A.1: Dataset Scenes and Objects

The dataset encompasses three primary scenes: kitchen, living room, and tabletop settings.

It includes a variety of common household items, such as kitchen utensils, daily necessities, fruits and vegetables, drinks and snacks, stationery, books, clothing and toys.
